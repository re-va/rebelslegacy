Thank You Papa,
Thank You Maa,
While I Still Can…

This book began as a private act — a way to preserve memories, lessons, and gratitude while the people who shaped them are still here. It was written without an audience in mind, and only later considered for publication.

It is a book addressed first to parents, and only then to the world.

Some readers may recognize their own parents in these pages. Others may simply be reminded that time is limited, and words left unspoken do not wait.

This is not a self-help book. It offers no frameworks, prescriptions, or promised outcomes.

The book will be made available once editing is complete. There is no fixed release date.

How Publishing Works Here

Work published under Rebel’s Legacy is released when it is considered complete in intent and execution — not when visibility, algorithms, or external timelines demand it.

On Tools & Integrity

Tools, including AI, may be used to assist with language, structure, or clarity. Responsibility for meaning, accuracy, and voice always remains human.

Relationship With Readers

Readers are not treated as customers to be captured, but as individuals choosing to spend time with a piece of work.

A Quiet Commitment

Rebel’s Legacy will grow only if the work itself justifies expansion. Until then, restraint is a deliberate choice.

The Living Pact

We know the furious pace of today’s living. We understand the desperation of paying the next bill while keeping body, mind, and spirit together, not forgetting the family. We appreciate all readers connecting with us now, and the team of writers, illustrators, and editors who will become a part of this journey.

Originality & The Human Touch

We believe in the honest sharing of ideas and authored "original" work. Even where our authors have utilized AI as a tool, we require every soul intending to associate with us to ensure a "human touch" during proofreading and editing.

The Sanctity of Labor

Unauthorized reproduction hurts the authors and writers. It fails to fairly compensate them for their time and efforts. To us at Rebel’s Legacy Publishing, the theft of a creator’s labor is an unforgivable crime.
